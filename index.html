    Tesla Fan Club

    Founded in 2022, the Tesla Fan Club is a community of Tesla enthusiasts committed to advancing Tesla's mission of accelerating the transition to sustainable energy around the globe. Tesla club members support legislative efforts, provide test drives, volunteer at shows, educate existing and prospective owners, organize social events, and passionately refer friends and family to explore Tesla ownership.
